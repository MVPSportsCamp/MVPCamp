Who We Are

Built for Kids. Made to be Fun.

MVP Sports Camp is a fun, high-energy sports experience designed for kids ages 4 to 8. We keep things active, encouraging, and packed with laughs — your kid will build real skills, make great friends, and leave every session feeling like a champion.


Sessions run 1 or 2 hours and include soccer, wiffle ball, football, relay races, and plenty of other games. Every kid gets coached, cheered on, and celebrated.

The Story
